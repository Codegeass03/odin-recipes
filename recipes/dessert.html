RED VELVET CAKE RECIPE

Red velvet cake is a traditionally red xoloured layer cake, layered with icing.
It is basically a vanilla cake with a few tablespoons of cocoa powder and red food coloring mixed in.

INGREDIENTS
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons red food coloring
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ½ cups sifted all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon distilled white vinegar

STEPS
